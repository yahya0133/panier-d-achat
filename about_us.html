Pour Over

Water Temp: 195-205¬∞F
Brew Time: 2-3 minutes

1. Rinse filter and warm vessel

2. Add ground coffee

3. Pour hot water in circular motion

4. Allow to bloom for 30 seconds

5. Complete pour in stages

French Press

Grind Size: Coarse
Water Temp: 200¬∞F
Brew Time: 4 minutes

1. Add coarse ground coffee

2. Pour hot water

3. Stir gently

5. Press plunger slowly

Water Temp: 200¬∞F
